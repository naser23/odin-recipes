Simple Syrup

Description

if you enjoy many breakfast meals and like eating all homeade food then this is the recipe for you. you can put this atop your pancakes, waffles or anything else you like. no more dealing with Aunt Jemima

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup water

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan combine sugar and water. Bring to a boil, stirring, until sugar has dissolved. Allow to cool.
